Zone Logic

Zone 1

Zone 2

Partial coverage. Transition zone where terrestrial signals fade.

Zone 3

White Space. 0% terrestrial floor. Safe for uncoordinated deployment.

Identity Intensity Slider

This slider simulates Resource Congestion.

  • 0-50%: Spectrum is managed. NTN uses reserved PCI ranges to avoid collisions.
  • 51-100%: Spectrum is crowded. The simulator forces random "ID Theft" (Collision) where the NTN PCI replicates the underlying TN PCI.

Pilot Collision (Mod-3)

Even if PCIs are different, if they share the same Mod-3 value, their Reference Signals (RS) crash. In the Pilot Grid Analysis, this is shown as a striped red block. This causes high block error rates even with a strong signal.
