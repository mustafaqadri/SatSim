Network Coverage Zones (TN vs NTN)

Spectrum utilization boundaries for B5 and B' bands

Zone 1

(Within TELUS footprint)

Terrestrial Coverage Footprint

Both B5 and B' are fully used by TN and unavailable for use by NTN (AST).

Zone 2

(Buffer area)

B5 used by TN

Terrestrial Network's Edge

B' can be used for NTN (AST)

Zone 3

(Out of TELUS coverage)

NTN Cells (AST)

Both B5 and B' bands are available to use for NTN (AST).
